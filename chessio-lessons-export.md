## Level 0 – Meet the Board
Learn how to read ranks, files, and squares like e4.

> XP Reward: 10
> Slug: `level-0-lesson-1-board`

2. **Task 2:** Now tap the square a1. _(Select a1)_
3. **Task 3:** Tap the square h8. _(Select h8)_

2. **Task 2:** Move the rook from d4 to d1. _(Move d4 → d1)_

## Level 0 – How the Queen Moves
The queen is the most powerful piece — she moves like a rook AND a bishop!

> XP Reward: 15
> Slug: `level-0-lesson-4-queen`

1. **Task 1:** Move the queen from d1 to d8 (like a rook). _(Move d1 → d8)_
   - _Hint:_ The queen can move vertically — go all the way up the d-file.
2. **Task 2:** Move the queen from a1 to h8 (like a bishop). _(Move a1 → h8)_
   - _Hint:_ The queen can move diagonally — try the long diagonal.
3. **Task 3:** Move the queen from e4 to h7. _(Move e4 → h7)_
   - _Hint:_ Move diagonally toward the top-right corner.

---

## Level 0 – How the King Moves
The king is the most important piece — protect him at all costs! He moves one square in any direction.

> XP Reward: 15
> Slug: `level-0-lesson-5-king`

1. **Task 1:** Move the king from e1 to e2. _(Move e1 → e2)_
   - _Hint:_ The king can only move one square — go forward.
2. **Task 2:** Move the king from d4 to e5 (diagonally). _(Move d4 → e5)_
   - _Hint:_ Move diagonally up and to the right.
3. **Task 3:** Move the king from h8 to g7. _(Move h8 → g7)_
   - _Hint:_ Move diagonally down and to the left.

---

## Level 0 – How the Pawn Moves
Pawns are humble but powerful — they can only move forward, but they have a special first-move option!

> XP Reward: 15
> Slug: `level-0-lesson-6-pawn`

1. **Task 1:** Move the pawn from e2 to e4 (two squares on first move). _(Move e2 → e4)_
   - _Hint:_ From the starting position, a pawn can jump two squares forward.
2. **Task 2:** Move the pawn from d4 to d5 (one square forward). _(Move d4 → d5)_
   - _Hint:_ Pawns move straight forward — one square only after the first move.
3. **Task 3:** Move the pawn from a7 to a8 (promotion square!). _(Move a7 → a8)_
   - _Hint:_ Move the pawn to the last rank — something special happens there!
