## Timing Strategy

### Recommended: Monday Start

**Why Monday:**
- Clear start of the week = easy to remember
- People have energy after the weekend
- Weekly mission cadence aligns with calendar weeks

**Sample Timeline:**

**Saturday (2 days before):**
- Send all invites
- Give people weekend to create account + join Discord
- Post welcome message in #welcome-start-here

**Sunday (1 day before):**
- Check who joined Discord
- Welcome them in #club-chat
- Remind them: "Mission starts tomorrow!"

**Monday (Day 1):**
- Post Season 01 announcement in #announcements
- Post Week 1 mission right after
- Drop nudge in #club-chat

### Alternative: Weekend Start

If your members are busy weekdays:

**Friday evening:**
- Send invites
- Post Season announcement

**Saturday morning:**
- Post Week 1 mission
- People have weekend to start

**Adjust weekly cadence to Friday missions instead of Monday**
