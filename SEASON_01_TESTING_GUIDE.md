## Common Issues & Quick Fixes

### Issue: Week 1 Mission Takes 45+ Minutes

**Fix options:**
1. Adjust messaging: "Week 1 mission: 30-45 min (2-3 sessions)"
2. Remove one lesson from Week 1
3. Keep as-is but warn: "Some people finish in 20 min, others take 45"

### Issue: Discord Feels Empty/Quiet

**This is normal for 5-10 people.** Don't panic.

**Fix:**
- Post more "permission to participate" messages
- Share your own positions/puzzles first
- Ask specific questions: "What was one thing you learned today?"

### Issue: Members Don't Know When to Post

**Fix:**
- Add to welcome message: "Feel free to post anytime â€“ before, during, or after lessons"
- Model it yourself: Post as you go, not just after completing missions

### Issue: Hints in App Aren't Helpful

**Fix for Sprint 05:**
- Note which tasks have bad hints
- Rewrite them based on actual confusion
- Consider visual hints (highlight squares)

### Issue: You Feel Overwhelmed by Daily Checks

**Fix:**
- Set Discord to "Do Not Disturb" except during your scheduled check time
- Remove Discord from phone (desktop only)
- Remind yourself: "5 min, that's it"

## What NOT to Test

**Don't obsess over:**
- Perfect copy (good enough is fine)
- Visual polish (already done in v1.3-alpha)
- Edge cases (wait for real users to hit them)
- Scale (you're testing for 5-10 people, not 1000)

**Don't add features based on testing.**  
Only fix things that would genuinely block a beginner.
