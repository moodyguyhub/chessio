## Launch Strategy: Soft & Controlled

### Phase 1: First Wave (Week 1)

**Who to invite:**
- 5‚Äì10 people you personally know
- People who are:
  - Curious about chess/learning
  - Not super demanding
  - Will give honest feedback (not just praise)
  - Represent diverse skill levels (true beginners ‚Üí rusty players)

**What to send:**
- One DM with:
  - Chessio app link (https://chessio.io)
  - Discord invite link
  - Short context (alpha + Season 01)
  - Clear expectations (experiment, 20-30 min/week, honest feedback)

**When to send:**
- Pick a **Monday** as "Season 01 Day 1"
- Send invites **2 days before** (Saturday) so people can join over the weekend
- Example: If Season starts Monday Dec 9, send invites Saturday Dec 7

### Phase 2: Mid-Season (Optional)

**If first wave goes well:**
- Add 5‚Äì10 more people in Week 2 or 3
- Same criteria: trusted, diverse skill levels
- Tell them: "You're joining mid-season, catch up at your own pace"

**If first wave struggles:**
- Pause invites
- Fix what's broken
- Resume in Season 02

### Phase 3: Season 02

**Based on Season 01 learnings:**
- Adjust missions/timing
- Improve confusing lessons
- Add features from feedback
- Scale to 20‚Äì30 members if sustainable

## What to Watch (Without Obsessing)

You don't need Perfect Analytics‚Ñ¢. Just watch these 4 signals:

### 1. **Showing Up**
**Signal:** A few messages per week in Discord  
**Good:** 3+ people posting in #club-chat or #wins-and-reflections  
**Concerning:** Total silence for 3+ days  
**Action if concerning:** Post a gentle nudge: "Anyone trying the mission? Share your progress!"

### 2. **Getting Stuck**
**Signal:** Same question/confusion appearing multiple times  
**Good:** Different people asking different questions  
**Concerning:** 3+ people confused about the same thing  
**Action if concerning:** 
- Answer publicly in #club-chat
- Log to Atlas: "Common blocker ‚Äì [issue]"
- Flag for Sprint 05

### 3. **Feeling Pressured**
**Signal:** Messages with guilt/shame language  
**Good:** "I only did 2 lessons this week but learned X"  
**Concerning:** "Sorry I'm behind, I'm terrible at this"  
**Action if concerning:**
- Reply publicly: "No apologies needed! Season 01 is about finding what works, not being perfect."
- Soften copy in next week's mission
- Check your own language ‚Äì are you accidentally adding pressure?

### 4. **Feeling Progress**
**Signal:** People sharing wins, no matter how small  
**Good:** "I finally spotted a fork before moving!" or "I reached Pawn level!"  
**Concerning:** No one sharing any wins after 2 weeks  
**Action if concerning:**
- Post in #club-chat: "What's ONE thing you learned this week, even if it's tiny?"
- Celebrate the smallest wins yourself: "Someone just told me they learned what a pin is ‚Äì that's the stuff ü•ã"

## Emergency Procedures

### If Discord Gets Too Noisy
- Create `#quiet-zone` channel for focused discussion
- Use threads more aggressively
- Set #club-chat to "slow mode" (1 message per 30 seconds)

### If People Drop Off Completely
- Send a **private DM** (not public guilt):
  ```
  Hey! Noticed you've been quiet in the club. Everything okay?
  
  No pressure at all ‚Äì just checking if there are any blockers
  or if Season 01 timing just doesn't work right now.
  ```
- Don't guilt-trip. Just check in.
- If they say "too busy" ‚Üí totally fine, invite them to Season 02

### If Someone Asks About Paid Features
- Response template:
  ```
  Right now we're focused on making the core experience great.
  
  Pricing will come later ‚Äì for Season 01, everything is free
  and your feedback is the most valuable thing.
  ```

### If Technical Bug Blocks Progress
- Ask them to use in-app feedback button
- Log the issue in a private `#founder-notes` channel (your eyes only)
- If it's critical: fix immediately
- If it's minor: log for Sprint 05

### If You're Overwhelmed
- **Cut daily Discord checks to every other day**
- Post in #club-chat:
  ```
  Hey everyone ‚Äì taking a breather for 2 days.
  
  Keep going with the missions, help each other out,
  and I'll be back [day] to catch up.
  ```
- Members will understand. Founder burnout helps no one.

## Energy Management

### Signs You're Doing It Right
- You check Discord once/day without anxiety
- You can skip a day without guilt
- You're excited (not stressed) on Mission Day
- You're learning interesting things about how people learn chess

### Signs You're Overdoing It
- Checking Discord every hour
- Feeling guilty when not "engaging"
- Trying to reply to every message
- Staying up late to "monitor" the club

**If you notice overdoing signs:**
1. Set Discord to "Do Not Disturb" except during your scheduled check times
2. Remove Discord from your phone (desktop only)
3. Remind yourself: "Season 01 is an experiment, not a commitment to 24/7 support"
